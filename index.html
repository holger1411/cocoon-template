Frequently asked questions

How long does setup take?
Most teams are up and running in under 10 minutes. Connect your repo, configure your build settings, and push — that's it. No infrastructure to provision.
Can I use my own domain?
Yes. Add your custom domain in the dashboard and we handle SSL certificates automatically. Wildcard domains are supported on Pro and Enterprise plans.
What happens when I exceed the free tier limits?
We'll notify you before you hit any limits. Your projects will continue to run — we never take your site down. You can upgrade or optimize at your own pace.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes. If you're not happy within the first 30 days of a paid plan, we'll refund you in full — no questions asked.
